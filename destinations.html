Our Featured Destinations

Palawan

Known for its limestone cliffs, crystal-clear lagoons, and breathtaking beaches, Palawan is often referred to as the last frontier of the Philippines. It's perfect for diving, island hopping, and exploring nature.

Best Time to Visit:

The dry season from December to May is the best time to visit Palawan for sunny weather and calm seas.

Learn More About Palawan

Siargao

Siargao, known as the surfing capital of the Philippines, offers more than just great waves. It's a paradise for adventure seekers with its lush landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and laid-back atmosphere.

Things to Do:

  • Surfing at Cloud 9
  • Island hopping to nearby islets
  • Explore Sugba Lagoon
Learn More About Siargao

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are over 2,000 years old, a masterpiece of ancient Filipino engineering. These terraces have been carved into the mountains by indigenous Ifugao people and are a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Why Visit?

Visitors come to Banaue not only to see the terraces but to learn about the rich culture of the Ifugao people and their traditional farming methods.
